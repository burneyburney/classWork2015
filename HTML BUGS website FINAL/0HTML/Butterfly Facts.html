Butterflies.. Useful or Useless?

Purpose

 Butterflies carry pollen from plant to plant, helping fruits, vegetables, and flowers to produce new seeds

4 Quick Facts

 Butterflies live on an all-liquid diet
Butterflies can not fly if they are cold
Butterflies live for 2-4 weeks on average
Butterflies are found worldwide except on Antarctica
