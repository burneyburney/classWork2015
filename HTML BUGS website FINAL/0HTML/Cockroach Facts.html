Cockroaches.. Useful or Useless?

Purpose

 Cockroaches are "middle men" consuming dead organic material while providing food for many animals such as bats, rodents, scorpions, spiders etc...

4 Quick Facts
Cockroaches can run up to 1.8 mph
A cockroach can hold it's breath for up to 40 minutes
A cockroach can live for up to a month, without its head
The favourite food of the cockroach is actually the glue on envelopes
