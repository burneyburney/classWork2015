Snails.. Useful or Useless?

Purpose

 Snails are decomposers. They help break down dead things.

4 Quick Facts

 A snail can sleep for three years
The slime a snail leaves behind allows it to easily move across any type of terrain without injuring its body
Snails can produce both sperm and eggs
Average life span of a snail is ~15 years; although it is believed that some species live up to 25 years.
